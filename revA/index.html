Tim Duffy attended school at Rochester Institute of Technology for Computer Engineering Technology, and is an currently working as a Systems Engineer at Viewpoint Systems designing embedded hardware and software. Tim is also a co-founder of Hacks/Hackers Rochester and an avid Civic Hacker in his free time. He has worked on numerous Open Source projects including MCSafetyFeed (real-time 911 feed for Monroe County, NY), Monroe Minutes (Town meeting minutes agrigator and search engine for Monroe County, NY), BarkingOwl (Web scraper/document discovery tool), and YellR ( Community engagement/Citizen Journalist mobile app and website).
